Club info

Lagarto Athletic Clube, commonly known as Lagarto, is a Azerbaijanian football club based in Baku, Azerbaijan. Lagarto is currently considered one of the best mini-football teams in Baku, at 10th place overall. The club was found in 2021 by some students from Baku Business University, Azerbaijan State Economic University, and Ozyegin University(Turkey). They are called "Lagartos" by their fans. "Lagartos" plays their games in "Azfar" stadium company. Their main target is to have a win streak. Their colors are considered green, black, and red, but they can use different colors in their uniforms. In main uniform, they use green as main, red as second Currently, and black as third color. the team has 12 players in squad (6 main and 6 subs). They also use lizzard (Lagarto means lizzard) and wave(Caspian Sea's wave) atributes in their uniforms. The team uses "Carnaval De Paris" as Club Anthem.
